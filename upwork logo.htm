A New Brand Identity

Don't ask how.
Ask who.

With Upwork, clients and talent have access to a work marketplace that helps scale their ambition and the strategic, functional tools that allow them to do so. Our new brand identity balances the duality of Upwork’s brand promise. On the one side: warmth and connectivity. On the other: precision and efficiency.

Wordmark

There’s only one way to go when we build on each other’s ideas.

When it comes to Upwork’s logo, we maintain elements with established brand equity, like the iconic “U” and “P” ligature, which communicate a sense of connection and upward momentum. Refined letterforms also give the logo a more modern look. The result is a bolder, more confident logo.

Typography

When we work together, there's nothing we can't do.

Our typography further articulates the dynamic nature of Upwork’s brand promise. Utilizing Upwork RZA, we communicate our warm and human side, and relay a conversational tone intended for personal communications and headlines. Upwork Neue Montreal demonstrates efficiency and precision, and is for use in technical headlines, detailed typography, and technical information. The system, like Upwork, is the perfect balance of warmth and utility.

Color

Ignore how it's always been done.

For our color palette, we forgo the more commercial tradition of leveraging a single color in favor of a modern solution that remains flexible when communicating across different channels. Built around our primary colors, Up Green and Up Lime, supporting secondary and tertiary colors create a robust palette that’s energetic and surprising, allowing for a wide range of expression.

Illustration

Inspired professionals inspire professionals.

A system of expressive, imaginative illustrations and iconography bring Upwork to life in both creative and practical ways. In addition to communicating Upwork’s functional core benefits, like the ability to find talent all over the world, the illustrations can tap into our more conceptual ideas, such as unlocking our potential and moving more freely.

Photography

From all corners of the world. From all worlds of thought.

Upwork’s photography reflects our essential role in facilitating the needs of both clients and independent talent and celebrates a modern workforce diverse in age, ethnicity, and gender. No longer bound to the office, today’s talent works from anywhere, on any time zone. We use natural lighting to document real people in real environments, stripping away artificial elements so the humanity of our subjects shines.
